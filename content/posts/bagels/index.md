# Dependencies

## Tools

- [ ] Oven
- [ ] Cooktop
- [ ] Clean work surface
- [ ] Stand mixer + bread hook

## Ingredients

> Measure by weight for optimal results

- [ ] **850g** bread flour
- [ ] **15g** brown sugar
- [ ] **35mL** olive oil
- [ ] **450mL** warm water (~40° ± 2°)
- [ ] **12g** salt
- [ ] **14g** instant yeast (2 standard packets)

### Boil

- [ ] **1000mL** water
- [ ] **20mL** honey
- [ ] **10g** salt 

### Egg Wash

- [ ] **1 egg**
- [ ] **15mL** water

### Additional items:

- [ ] All-purpose flour for your work surface + fixing the dough if need be)
- [ ] Arbitrary amount of fancy-ass salt or cinnamon sugar or something for topping.
- [ ] Additional olive oil for bowl

# Make dough

1. **Distribute yeast** in flour manually or with breadhook attachment
2. **Add and combine** water, oil, and sugar into the mixture at low until moistened.
3. **Add** salt until combined.
3. **Mix** on medium-low speed for 6 minutes.
